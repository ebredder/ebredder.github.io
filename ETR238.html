This class served as an introduction to Stepper Motors and a review of the Arduino platform. We set up a simple controller utilizing ULN2003 and L293D motor drivers to control 8-phase, geared stepper motor: 28BYJ-48. Our goals in this class were tiered as follows:

Level 1: Use the Arduino platform and one of the two driver choices to perform a full rotation clockwise and counter-clockwise.

Level 2: Use the Arduino platform, one of the drivers, and a pushbutton or potentiometer as an input to control start/stop and rotation of the stepper motor.

Level 3: Use the Arduino platform, one driver, a pushbutton/potentiometer, and a hall effect sensor to gain feedback/control of the stepper position.
